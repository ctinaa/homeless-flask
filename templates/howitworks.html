Mission and Purpose


Living on the streets, is not pretty.

Unsure of where to sleep the next day is intiminating.

Not knowing where your next meal is coming from, if at all, is crushing.



But if there was a place to start a new life.

A place to share thier story.

A place to reach out to the world.





With some work and a lot of effort, anyone can advance themselves.


Homeless Hub is a non-profit service that gives homeless people another chance to make a living for themselves. By allowing homeless people to communicate with possible donors in a way that is not intimidating or looked down upon. We strive to change the stigma against homelessness. In addition to breaking down barriers and unecessary social prejudice, we aim to decrease America's unemployment rate.

This web app was created for homeless persons to share there stories. The stories are then processed and the strongest/weakest skills are displayed visually. A profile is then created from these stories, skills, and additional information. This profile will be sent to a sister website where donors can fund the profile's training. The progress of funding can be veiwed on this web page.
